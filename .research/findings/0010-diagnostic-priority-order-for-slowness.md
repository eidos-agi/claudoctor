## Claim

Community consensus diagnostic checklist for slow Claude Code: (1) session age — is history bloated? (2) .claudeignore — are large files visible? (3) model/context size — right-sized for task? (4) CLI process health — CPU/RAM/Node version? (5) timeout/retry/compression config. This order should inform claudoctor's check priority.
