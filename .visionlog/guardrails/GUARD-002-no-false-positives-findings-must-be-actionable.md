title: "No false positives \u2014 findings must be actionable"
status: "active"
date: "2026-04-03"
---

Every finding must have: what's wrong, why it matters, and what to do about it. If a check can't explain the impact, it shouldn't fire. Noisy diagnostics get ignored. Precision over recall.
